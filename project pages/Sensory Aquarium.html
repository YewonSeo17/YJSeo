Sensory Aquarium

Oct 27, 2023

This underwater structure is designed as an aquarium specifically for the visually impaired. The pathways inside are dark, and shallow water flows through them. As visitors walk along the pathways, they can experience marine life by feeling the cold water with their bare feet and listening to the sounds made by sea creatures. By removing the sense of vision, visitors can have a unique and interactive multi-sensory experience that is different from traditional aquariums.
