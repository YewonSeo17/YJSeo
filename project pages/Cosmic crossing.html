Cosmic crossing

Sep 15, 2023

The title “Cosmic crossing” means the horizon where the ocean meets the sky. Co-workers and I designed the structure to show the process of take-off and landing that astronauts experience in their every flight. While visitors climb stairs, they observe the concrete mass that gradually turns into the horizon. It represents the landing of a spaceship. Meanwhile, after getting out of the concrete walls, visitors climb the ramp to the park and resist gravity. This cosmic experience harmonizes with visitors and makes a unique impression. In addition, it offers an opportunity to learn the history of space science.
