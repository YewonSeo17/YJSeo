Serene Chaos

Fab 1, 2024

Every individual cloaks their anxiety deeply beneath their mind. In my case, anxiety appears as a destructive image of wounded stationarity. It could be the shape of a sculpture patched with lumps of paper clay, or it could seem like a precarious boat on a fluctuating ocean surface. These two pieces of artwork will help viewers encounter their anxiety blocking their authentic selves to express ego.
